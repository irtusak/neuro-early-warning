Interview Prep: NeuroWatch Talking Points

Ready ▶

When asked "tell me about a project" — use these five points:

01
The Problem
NB mystery illness went undetected for months because no system was watching for spatial clusters of rare neurological symptoms.
02
What You Built
Three independent AI detectors that cross-validate each other, reducing false alarms (FDR-controlled at 5%).
03
Why It Matters
The system found the NB cluster automatically with HIGH confidence — exactly the kind of early signal that was missed in real life.
04
The Policy Layer
You didn't just build a detector — you added environmental risk scoring and socioeconomic vulnerability analysis because health threats don't exist in isolation from their determinants.
05
Expert Validation
The system was refined against Health Canada expert feedback — privacy, statistical rigor, data leakage prevention.

Your Edge

  • Most MPH students don't enter with a deployed, functional surveillance tool
  • You sit at the intersection of health informatics + epidemiology + policy — a high-demand niche
  • You have both the technical ability to build AND the domain knowledge to contextualize
  • NeuroWatch is not a class assignment — it's a working system that addresses a real public health gap
