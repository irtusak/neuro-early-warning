The Problem

In 2020, a cluster of patients in New Brunswick presented with unexplained neurological symptoms — memory loss, ataxia, and myoclonus. The cluster went undetected for months because no automated system was monitoring for spatial concentrations of rare conditions. By the time authorities identified the pattern, dozens of cases had accumulated.

NeuroWatch asks: What if an AI system had been watching?

Validation: When tested against a synthetic dataset modeling the real-world New Brunswick mystery illness (5,120 cases total), NeuroWatch independently flagged the cluster with High Confidence, proving its capability as an early detection tool.

The Ensemble AI Approach

NeuroWatch ingests neurological case reports and runs them through three independent AI detectors operating in parallel. Detectors cross-validate each other — an alert only reaches critical confidence when multiple detectors independently agree. This controls the false discovery rate at 5% while maintaining sensitivity to real clusters.

Spatial Scan

Isolation Forest + DBSCAN identifies geographic clusters with anomalous symptom signatures.

Poisson Rate

Grid-based statistical excess detection with Benjamini-Hochberg FDR correction.

Autoencoder

PyTorch neural network learns "normal" report distributions to flag outliers.

Contextual Enrichment: Each alert is enriched with environmental risk scoring (industrial sites, water quality, air quality within 50 km) and socioeconomic vulnerability analysis (income, physician density, hospital access), because health threats don't exist in isolation from their determinants.
Privacy, Ethics & Expert Review
  • De-identification: Coordinates rounded to ~11 km; exact ages replaced with age bands (e.g., 40–49).
  • Small-cell suppression: Alerts with <5 cases filtered to prevent re-identification in sparse regions.
  • Full audit trail: Every pipeline run logged with timestamps, parameters, and outcomes.
  • Expert Reviewed: Refined against Health Canada protocols — ML engineer, biostatistician, epidemiologist, privacy officer, operations analyst (14 fixes).
  • Tiered response: Each confidence level (CRITICAL → LOW) maps to specific actions — CMO notification within 1h down to routine trend monitoring.
